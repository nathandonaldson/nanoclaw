## Key People

- **Blair Strong** (WithKnives, Dunedin) — Chef by trade, runs WithKnives as a side hustle (open every second Saturday). Returned to full-time work 2025 after retail dropped off. Hasn't been to Japan in 6 years. Can source honyaki through Sakai Kikumori/Tanaka. Thinks Makoto Kurosaki makes better-cutting knives than brother Yu.
- **Nakagawa Satoshi** — Apprenticed under Kenichi Shiraki for 16 years, took over and rebranded as Nakagawa Uchihamono April 2021. One of ~4 blacksmiths worldwide who can make mizu-honyaki from Shirogami #1. Nathan inquiring about a honyaki gyuto commission via KIREAJI.
- **Yoshikazu Ikeda** — President of Sakai Uchihamono Traditional Craftsmen Association, certified 1988. One of fewer than 10 active honyaki makers in Japan.
